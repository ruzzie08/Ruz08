About Me

Khent Ruzzel Uy Roxas was born on July 26, 2003, at United Doctors Medical Center Quezon City Metro Manila Philippines. He is the third of the siblings, He grew up in the City of Pasig for 15 years. He graduated from Senior High School in Pasig Catholic College (PCC) with a strand of Humanities and Social Sciences in his alma mater he was known as one of the best students in their school because of his unique ability to be a Leader of an organization, he also serves the Regimental commander in Citizen Advanced Training (CAT) in High School. and now, He is a student at Northwest Samar State University (NwSSU) with a program of Bachelor of Science in Information Technology he is in 2nd-year level in college, He is also a member officer with the rank of Cadet Captain of Reserve Officer Training Corps (ROTC) in the same institute, he is also known as "The Punisher" due to his strict and integrity leadership as a ROTC Officer.
